Biography of Chief

 Jamilah Watkins is the founder and owner of Sea/Soul Cuisine; before she became a entrepreneur, she obtained a Bachelors Degree in Liberal Arts as well as a Registered Property Manager (RAM) certification; she now has over 13 years of experience the industry of property management, this includes the overseeing and managing over 60 residential buildings and over 2,000 apartment units.

Chief Watkins grew up in the humble streets of Brooklyn, NY, where her passion for cooking was molded by her grandmother. Every time her grandmother was in the kitchen she would assist her in preparing the Sunday dinners; dishes from the Caribbean to down south southern cuisine. The recipes created in Sea/Soul Cuisine were inspired from generations upon generations.

Where It All Started..

 Jamilah Watkins started the Sea/Soul Cuisine Restaurant from a catering business; from which she would cater for small events such as birthday parties, baby showers, officer parties, engagement parties, Thanksgiving and Christmas parties. Now her business has boomed and while she still dapples in property management; her restaurant is her passion and main focus.
