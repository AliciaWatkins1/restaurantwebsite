Our Story

Jamilah Watkins started the Sea/Soul Cuisine Restaurant from a catering business; from which she would cater for small events such as birthday parties, baby showers, officer parties, engagement parties, Thanksgiving and Christmas parties.

Jamilah Watkins is the founder and owner of Sea/Soul Cuisine; before she became a entrepreneur, she obtained a Bachelors Degree in Liberal Arts as well as a Registered Property Manager (RAM) certification; she now has over 13 years of experience the industry of property management, this includes the overseeing and managing over 60 residential buildings and over 2,000 apartment units.

Location:

Sea/Soul Cuisine Restaurant is located in Harlem on 125th street between 7th and 8th Avenue (right across Black Ink Tattoo Shop) Harlem is famous for their basketball team called the Harlem Globe Trotters and the Apollo Theatre as well as other movies that were film there such as: Paid in Full, New Jack City and Killa Season.
