Our Story

We serve the best fried seafood, soulfood, and rasta pasta in Brooklyn and we are here to stay. We are making a grass-roots effort to begin a new trend in Take-Out dining with the best fried seafood, soulfood, and rasta pasta in Harlem.

We have become a leading force in the Fast Casual Dining scene in Manhattan. We at Sea/Soul Cuisine strive to put the best in all our soul food and fried seafood dishes which consist of the freshest ingredients. Blessed by the chief in the kitchen, our crew is loaded with knowledgeable individuals for all your needs.

Location:

Sea/Soul Cuisine Restaurant is located in Harlem on 125th street between 7th and 8th Avenue (right across Black Ink Tattoo Shop) Harlem is famous for their basketball team called the Harlem Globe Trotters and the Apollo Theatre as well as other movies that were film there such as: Paid in Full, New Jack City and Killa Season.

Our Mission Statement

  1. To sell delicious and remarkable food and drinks.


  2. The food and drinks we sell meets the highest standards of quality, freshness and seasonality and combines both modern-creative and traditional southern styles of cooking.


  3. To consistently provide our customers with impeccable service by demonstrating warmth, graciousness, efficiency, knowledge, professionalism and integrity in our work.


  4. To have every customers who comes through our doors leave impressed by and excited to come back again.
