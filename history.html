Sea/Soul Cuisine's Humble Beginnings

 Sea/Soul Cuisine is a Family owned restaurant where everyone is welcomed from all walks of life. Sea/Soul Cuisine opened it's doors on August 21, 2016, with a large amount of clientele from Chief Watkins catering business. Celebrities like Jay-Z, Beyonce, Reggae Dancehall Artist (Alkaline), Retried Basketball Player Michael Jordan; as well as paparazzi and a blue carpet. This restaurant has only been open 8 months and since then it has become the go to place for fine dining and old fashioned home cooked meals.

"Although the skills aren't hard to learn, finding the happiness and finding the satisfaction and finding fulfillment in continuously serving somebody else something good to eat, is what makes a really good restaurant." -Jamilah Watkins
